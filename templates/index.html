Examples

  • म नेपाल मा बस्छु ।
    • I live in Nepal
  • बाबु राम भट्टराई नेपाल का हुन् ।
    • Babu Ram Bhattarai is from Nepal
  • पुष्प कमल दाहाल एमाओवादी का अद्यक्ष्य हुन्।
    • Pushpa Kamal Dahal is the president of CPN Maoist

Notes

  • Only Nepali language is accepted as input
  • Recognizes only three types of entities Person, Organization and Location
  • PER = Person, ORG = Organization, LOC = Location
  • B-Per = Beginning of Person, I-PER = Inside of Person
